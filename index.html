Common questions

Why is no email sent?
Alive uses your device’s Mail app to send messages. If no Mail account is configured on your device, emails cannot be sent.

Does Alive contact emergency services?
No. Alive never contacts emergency services. Emails are sent only to the emergency contact chosen by the user and only after user confirmation.

How is location used?
Location is accessed only when the user taps “Help Now” and is included in the email sent to the emergency contact. Alive does not track location in the background.
